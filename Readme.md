# 🎹 Web Harmonium – Play Harmonium Online

*A Free Virtual Indian Harmonium You Can Play in Your Browser*

Play a **realistic harmonium online** with keyboard controls, authentic sound, and a modern interface.
Perfect for **beginners, musicians, and learners of Indian classical music**.

## 🎼 How to Play

### Keyboard Layout

```
Black Keys:  1  2     4  5     7  8  9     -  =
White Keys: ` q w e r t y u i o p [ ] \
```

---

### 🎶 Notes (Sargam)

* Sa → Root
* Re → Movement
* Ga → Emotion
* Ma → Transition
* Pa → Stability
* Dha → Depth
* Ni → Leading tone
